Questions and Answers

  1. What is the Global Tech Conference?
    2. A three-day annual event bringing together professionals, researchers, and students to explore innovation, technology trends, and networking opportunities.
  2. Where is the venue located?
    3. The conference will take place at the Bengaluru International Convention Centre.
  3. Do I need to register in advance?
    4. Yes, advance registration is required to secure your spot and receive conference materials.
  4. What are the conference dates?
    5. 12–14 December 2025.
  5. Are there any participation fees?
    6. Yes, the registration fee varies depending on your category (student, professional, or speaker). Details are available on the registration page.
  6. Will sessions be recorded?
    7. Selected sessions will be recorded and shared with registered participants after the event.
  7. Can I attend workshops without registering for the full conference?
    8. No, workshops are part of the full conference package and require prior registration.
  8. Is accommodation provided?
    9. The conference offers a list of recommended nearby hotels. Accommodation is not included in the registration fee.
  9. Are meals included?
    10. Yes, lunch and coffee breaks are included for all registered attendees.
